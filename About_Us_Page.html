About Us

Commencing in 1972 in Ahmedabad, Chiripal Group embarked on its journey with a handful of power looms. Swiftly conquering the polyester yarn domain, the group diversified into cotton spinning and denim fabric, solidifying its status as Asia’s premier denim manufacturer. Pillars in the textile and petrochemical verticals include Nandan Denim, Chiripal Industries, Vishal Fabrics, Nandan Terry, and CIL Nova Petrochemicals. The newest addition, Nandan Terry, has swiftly gained acclaim in terry towels. In flexible packaging, Chiripal Poly Films Limited (2012) holds its ground. The infrastructure arm boasts Vraj Integrated Textile Park (2005), Dholi Integrated Spinning Park (2007), and Shanti Developers. In education, Shanti Juniors, Shanti Asiatic School, and Shanti Business School underscore the group’s commitment to shaping future leaders. Venturing into education with Shanti Educational Initiatives in 2009 reflects Chiripal Group’s strategic dedication to community welfare.

Achievements
Founding Visionary Era
1972–1993
Chiripal Industries emerged in 1972 with 12 power looms in Ahmedabad, focusing on textile trading and polyester yarn manufacturing.
Textile Dominance
2004–2016
Diversifying into cotton spinning and denim weaving propelled Chiripal Group to become India’s largest denim manufacturer.
Packaging Eminence
2009–2015
Chiripal Polyfilms rose as the third-largest packaging manufacturer in India, producing BOPP and BOPET.
Educational Foray
2010–2014
Chiripal expanded into education with Shanti Juniors, Shanti Asiatic School, and Shanti Business School.
Towel Artistry
2016–2018
Establishing Nandan Terry Ltd., Chiripal Group ventured into the terry towel textile segment.
Financial Prowess
2016
The group diversified into non-banking finance by launching VITA Loan.
