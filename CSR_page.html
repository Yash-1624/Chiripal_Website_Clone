Chiripal Mirchi Green Yodha

The "Chiripal Mirchi Green Yodha" is an environmental initiative launched by the Chiripal Group in collaboration with Radio Mirchi. This campaign focuses on promoting tree plantation and e-waste management in Ahmedabad, aiming to enhance the city’s green cover and foster community involvement in environmental sustainability. The campaign, which commenced on World Environment Day, has successfully planted thousands of trees across various locations, including schools, societies, and public spaces.

Chiripal Women's Day Celebrations

Chiripal Group celebrates International Women's Day by emphasizing the importance of female empowerment, gender equality, and the recognition of women's contributions across all spheres of life. The group honors the strength, resilience, and achievements of women, reaffirming its commitment to fostering an inclusive environment where women can thrive both personally and professionally. Through its initiatives, Chiripal Group advocates for the advancement of women's rights and continuously strives to create opportunities that support and uplift women within the organization and the broader community.

Cricket Premier League

The Chiripal Foundation's Cricket Premier League (CPL) is a dynamic T20 cricket tournament that showcases and promotes local talent in Gujarat, India. This highly anticipated event not only serves as a platform for emerging cricketers to demonstrate their skills but also fosters community spirit and enthusiasm for the sport.
